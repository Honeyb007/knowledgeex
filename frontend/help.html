How do I book a tutoring session?
Go to Find Tutors from your dashboard. Browse or search by course code or name. Click Book Session, fill in the subject, an optional message, and your preferred date — then confirm. Your payment locks into escrow automatically via the smart contract.
What happens after I book a session?
Your booking enters Pending status. The tutor has 48 hours to accept or decline. Once accepted it becomes Scheduled. After the session, the tutor marks it complete — you then confirm to release payment from escrow to the tutor.
Can I cancel or reschedule a booking?
Tutors can decline pending bookings before accepting — your funds are returned automatically. Once scheduled, reach out to your tutor directly to reschedule. If the tutor doesn't respond within 48 hours, you can claim a refund via the smart contract on your Sessions page.
How do payments work on KnowledgeEx?
KnowledgeEx uses Ethereum smart contracts for all payments. When you book, the ETH is locked in an on-chain escrow. Funds are only released to the tutor after you confirm the session was completed — protecting both parties with zero middlemen.
What wallet do I need to pay?
You need MetaMask installed in your browser — it's a free digital wallet for Ethereum. Download it at metamask.io. Once installed, click Connect Wallet on your dashboard or wallet page to link it to KnowledgeEx.
What if I'm not satisfied with a session?
Payment stays locked in escrow until you confirm it. If unsatisfied, simply don't release the funds — and contact support with details. You can also leave an honest star rating so future learners know what to expect.
How do I become a tutor?
During sign-up, select I want to Teach (Tutor) as your role. You'll get a tutor dashboard where you can set up your profile, add courses, set your hourly rate and availability, then start receiving booking requests immediately.
How is tutor credibility determined?
Credibility is built on star ratings (1–5) and total sessions completed. After every session, learners can leave a rating and optional comment. The platform automatically calculates and displays the tutor's average — no admin verification needed.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
On the login page, click Forgot password? and enter your registered email. You'll receive a reset link valid for 30 minutes. Click it, enter a new password, and you're back in.
